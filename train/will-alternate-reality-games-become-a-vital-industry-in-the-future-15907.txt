Discussion Title: Will Alternate Reality Games Become A Vital Industry In The Future?

1. Alternate Reality Games will become a vital industry in the future
1.1. Pro: Every day, ARGs become more and more integrated into pop culture.
1.2. Pro: The medium that an ARG can be in is very flexible.
1.2.1. Pro: This allows people from a variety of backgrounds to access ARGs.
1.2.2. Pro: It has a variety of applications and possibilities that are only just being considered.
1.3. Pro: [An Alternate Reality Game That Takes Freshman Orientation to a New Level](https://www.wired.com/story/an-alternate-reality-game-that-takes-freshmen-orientation-to-a-new-level/)
1.3.1. Pro: Such examples are showing the wonderfully diverse ways ARG can be used; it is only just starting to be properly considered.
1.4. Con: ARGs can't be easily commercialized
1.4.1. Con: Some of the first ARGs were used to advertise for non-recreational products.
1.4.2. Pro: Majority of the current ARGs are non-profitable.
1.4.2.1. Con: This doesn't preclude the possibility of them becoming profitable; it is likely that commercial ventures will find ways to make use of such new technology.
1.4.2.2. Pro: They are too expensive.
1.4.3. Pro: A large portion of ARG players and creators are not fully dependent on them for money.
1.5. Con: ARGs require other industry products in order to exist.
1.5.1. Con: This isn't necessarily an impediment; it just means the rest of the industry would need to adapt, as it always does.
