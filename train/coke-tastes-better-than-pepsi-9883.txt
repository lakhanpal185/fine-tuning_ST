Discussion Title: Coke tastes better than Pepsi

1.3. Con: Pepsi is sweeter
1.4. Pro: Coke has a better brand image than Pepsi and therefore tastes better.
1.4.1. Pro: Coke spend more money on advertising
1.4.2. Pro: The better the image of a brand the better the perceived taste.
1.4.3. Con: there is no relation between sight and taste
1.5. Con: Pepsi is slightly cheaper; thus, when drinking one feels happier because one saved some money.
1.6. Con: Pepsi has a slightly higher [rating](https://www.diffen.com/difference/Coke_vs_Pepsi) than Coke.

1.7.2. Pro: Comparing Coke Zero with regular Pepsi is not possible since one contains sugar.
