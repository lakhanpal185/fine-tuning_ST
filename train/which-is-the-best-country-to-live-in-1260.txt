1.12. Bhutan
1.12.1. Con: Bhutan ranks just [84th](http://worldhappiness.report/wp-content/uploads/sites/2/2016/03/HR-V1Ch2_web.pdf) on the World Happiness Index.
1.12.2. Pro: Bhutan has a [varied climate](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Bhutan#Geography) with elevation, from subtropical in the south to temperate in the highlands and polar-type climate, with year-round snow in the north.
1.12.2.1. Pro: Bhutan even has [negative carbon-emissions](http://ecowatch.com/2016/03/19/bhutan-carbon-negative/).
1.12.3. Pro: The country orients itself by the [Gross National Happiness](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Gross_National_Happiness) index and thus focuses on the happiness of citizens.
