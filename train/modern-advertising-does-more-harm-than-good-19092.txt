Discussion Title: Modern advertising does more harm than good

1. Modern advertising does more harm than good
1.1. Pro: Adverts can heavily skew public opinion on what products and services are objectively "good".
1.1.1. Pro: Adverts represent a considerable competitive means of promoting products and services, which should be replaced with a purely factual means of discerning the "best" \(or scale of best-\>worst\) options for a given situation.
1.2. Pro: On many mediums, loose regulations mean adverts can be intentionally misleading and sensationalizing.
1.2.1. Pro: Merchants intentionally create a [false sense of urgency](https://marketingland.com/12-ways-use-urgency-psychology-improve-conversions-112603) in order to encourage consumers to 'buy now'.
1.2.2. Pro: It can be hard to discern when one is being advertised to rather than being presented with facts and accurate information on mediums that should be trustworthy \(news, informational websites etc\)
1.2.3. Pro: Adverts often use [sex and sexual innuendos](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Sex_in_advertising) to sell goods and services.
1.2.3.1. Pro: Women are often depicted as sexual objects, which can promote [body dissatisfaction](http://journals.sagepub.com/doi/10.1177/01461672992511012).
1.3. Pro: Adverts can use [subliminal messaging](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Subliminal_stimuli) to falsely engender a feeling of want/need where there is none.
1.3.1. Pro: Adverts use [color psychology](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Color_psychology#Uses_in_marketing) to visual stimulate and entice consumers.
1.4. Con: Modern advertising can help bring good causes into the mainstream public eye.
1.5. Con: Modern advertising can help promote new, helpful & useful products and services.
1.6. Con: Modern advertising can inform people of useful tools and services they were previously unaware of.
1.7. Con: Modern advertising can help small businesses grow.
1.7.1. Pro: Modern techniques, such as [guerrilla marketing](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Guerrilla_marketing), is a helpful tool to help small businesses make lasting impressions on its target market when up against big corporations.
1.8. Con: Modern advertising can improve the economy.
1.8.1. Pro: Modern techniques, such as e-marketing can attract more customers than older methods, meaning increased profits more money driving the economy forward.
1.9. Pro: Modern advertising is exaggerating inciting consumerism.
