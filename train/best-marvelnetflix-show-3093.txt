Discussion Title: Best Marvel/Netflix show?

1. Best Marvel/Netflix show
Also, please rank the shows using the impact bar \(4 for favorite through to 0 for least favorite\).
1.1. Iron Fist
1.1.1. Pro: [Madam Gao](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/The_Hand_\(comics\)) is the best villain
1.1.2. Pro: [Colleen Wing](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Colleen_Wing) is the best hero
1.1.3. Pro: Iron Fist has the most compelling plot.
1.1.4. Con: Iron Fist has been [heavily](https://www.theverge.com/2017/3/8/14848336/iron-fist-review-netflix-show-marvel) [criticised](https://thenerdsofcolor.org/2014/03/11/marvel-please-cast-an-asian-american-iron-fist/) for its racist [issues](https://www.vox.com/2016/2/26/11121378/iron-fist-racism).
1.2. The Defenders
1.2.1. Pro: The Defenders has the best plot.
1.2.2. Pro: The Defenders has all the heroes.
1.3. Luke Cage
1.3.1. Pro: [Cottonmouth](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Cottonmouth_\(Cornell_Stokes\)) is the best villain.
1.3.2. Pro: Luke Cage is the best hero.
1.3.3. Pro: Luke Cage has the best plot.
1.3.4. Pro: Luke Cage has the best [soundtrack](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Luke_Cage_\(soundtrack\)).
1.3.5. Pro: Luke Cage, stars a black hero and has a [diverse cast](https://www.polygon.com/2016/10/6/13177492/marvels-luke-cage-black-hero), thus it represent groups that are often marginalised on television.
1.4. Daredevil
1.4.1. Pro: Daredevil is the most compelling hero.
1.4.2. Pro: Daredevil has the best plot.
1.4.3. Pro: Daredevil was a [starting point](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Daredevil_\(TV_series\)#Marvel_Cinematic_Universe_tie-ins) for all the other series that followed.
1.4.4. Pro: Daredevil has the best choreographed fight sequences.
1.4.5. Pro: Daredevil is the best character study. Consider the parallels between Matt and Fisk vs Matt and Frank vs Matt and Elektra
1.4.6. Pro: Daredevil has [the Punisher](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Punisher).
1.4.6.1. Pro: Anti-heroes tend to be a bit more relatable than either pure heroes or villains.
1.4.7. Pro: [Wilson Fisk](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Kingpin_\(character\)) is the best villain.
1.5. Jessica Jones
1.5.1. Con: Jessica Jones was a lot darker in plot and tone than the others, and as such isn't as enjoyable to watch.
1.5.1.1. Con: A dark plot and tone does not make a show less enjoyable.
1.5.2. Pro: Jessica Jones is the most grounded show.
1.5.3. Con: The repeated \(failed\) attempts to defeat Kilgrave, while illustrating Jessica's ingenuity, were ultimately redundant.
1.5.4. Pro: [Killgrave](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Purple_Man) is the best villain.
1.5.4.1. Pro: David Tennant portrayed Killgrave brilliantly, giving him a special twist.
1.5.4.2. Con: The Killgrave version portrayed in Jessica Jones was a modified version of the original [Purple Man](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Purple_Man).
1.5.5. Pro: Jessica is the most compelling hero.
1.5.5.1. Pro: In contrast to most other 1-dimensional comic characters, Jessica seems to be a relatable human being with flaws.
1.5.5.2. Pro: It's refreshing for viewers to see a show about a heroine rather than a hero.
1.5.5.2.1. Pro: This is particularly appealing to female viewers, as it gives them a better opportunity to relate to the protagonist.
1.5.6. Pro: Jessica Jones has the best plot.
