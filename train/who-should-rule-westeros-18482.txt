Discussion Title: Who should rule Westeros?

1. Who should rule Westeros?
1.1. Stannis Baratheon
1.1.1. Pro: Stannis is a brother of Robert Baratheon, which gives him right to the throne.
1.1.2. Pro: Stannis is a strong ruler.
1.1.3. Con: Stannis has only one daughter, so his dynasty could easily extinguish.
1.2. Daenerys Stormborn of the House Targaryen, First of her Name, The Unburnt, Queen of the Andals and the First Men, Khaleesi of the Great Grass Sea, Breaker of Chains and the Mother of Dragons
1.2.1. Con: Daenerys has no family, her dynasty is incsecure.
1.2.1.1. Con: She was able to secure her place as queen. Its likely she's able to secure family in some way.
1.2.2. Pro: Daenerys is a woman. Most other rulers tend to be male.
1.2.2.1. Con: Cersie is a woman and rules King's Landing. Being a woman does not qualify a candidate for rulership.
1.2.3. Pro: Daenerys owns dragons, potentially securing Westeros for all eternity.
1.2.4. Pro: Daenerys is a descendant of Aerys II Targaryen, which makes her rightful heir of the throne.
1.2.5. Con: Having so much military power Daenerys could easily become a tyrant.
1.3. Tyrion Lannister
1.3.1. Pro: Tyrion is clever and apt for the role.
1.3.2. Pro: Tyrion has evolved in his leadership qualities.
1.4. Jon Snow
1.4.1. Pro: Jon snow is a good leader.
1.4.1.1. Pro: Jon Snow has proven he can choose what is in the best interest of everyone, not just himself or immediate kingdom.
1.4.2. Con: Jon Snow is believed to be Eddard's bastard, leaving him with no right to the throne.
1.5. The Night King
1.5.1. Pro: "I am the punishment of God. If you had not committed great sins, God would not have sent a punishment like me upon you" - [Genghis Khan](https://en.wikiquote.org/wiki/Genghis_Khan#Quotes). Westeros is full of bad people who've done bad things and who have it coming.
1.5.2. Pro: The Night King has been working towards his goals for longer than any of the other characters have been alive.
1.6. Eddard Stark
1.6.1. Con: Eddard was naive.
1.6.1.1. Pro: His naivety resulted in his death.
1.6.2. Pro: As a Lord of Winterfell Eddard showed his respect for law.
1.6.3. Pro: Eddard is a man of honor.
1.7. Cersei Lannister
1.7.1. Pro: Cersei has military power to secure her rule.
1.7.2. Pro: Cersei is a resourceful ruler and clever polititian.
1.7.3. Con: Cersei is cruel.
