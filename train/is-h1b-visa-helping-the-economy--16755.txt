Discussion Title: Is H1B visa helping the Economy ?

1. Is H1B visa helping the Economy ?
1.1. Pro: More than 40% of the recently created $1Bn firms had an immigrant founder. Most of these immigrant founders have come via the H1B program. [Read More](https://insights.som.yale.edu/insights/does-immigration-create-jobs)
1.2. Pro: Academic studies find that High Skilled Immigration has little to no effect [on employment levels](http://www8.nationalacademies.org/onpinews/newsitem.aspx?RecordID=23550)
1.3. Pro: Often times, [Immigrants take Jobs that U.S. Citizens don't want.](https://insights.som.yale.edu/insights/does-immigration-create-jobs)
1.3.1. Con: This is unlikely with the H1B visa. It pertains to "[specialty occupations](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/H-1B_visa#cite_note-4)" requiring theoretical and practical application of a body of highly specialized knowledge.
1.4. Pro: There is a lot of shortage of High Skilled workers. Especially [in Science, Technology, Engineering and Mathematics](https://insights.som.yale.edu/insights/does-immigration-create-jobs)
1.5. Con: 70% of the H1B Visa has been allocated to IT Services companies from [India](https://www.washingtonpost.com/news/wonk/wp/2017/04/03/this-one-group-gets-70-percent-of-high-skilled-foreign-worker-visas/?noredirect=on)
1.5.1. Con: Helping the booming US IT sector is good for the US economy.
1.5.1.1. Pro: Apple, Amazon, Facebook and Alphabet are able to register a strong growth.
1.6. Con: Large number of Indian workers has resulted in huge[Green Card  backlog](https://economictimes.indiatimes.com/nri/visa-and-immigration/indian-professionals-hold-rally-to-end-green-card-backlog-in-us/articleshow/63376365.cms) that puts pressure on the already broken immigration system
1.7. Con: Current policy is inadequate in handling [H1B visa abuse](https://www.uscis.gov/working-united-states/temporary-workers/h-1b-specialty-occupations-and-fashion-models/combating-fraud-and-abuse-h-1b-visa-program)
1.8. Con: American Workers feel some of their Jobs are vulnerable [to H1B Visa Programs](https://www.cbsnews.com/news/are-u-s-jobs-vulnerable-to-workers-with-h-1b-visas-2/)
