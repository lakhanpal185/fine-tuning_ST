Discussion Title: Is Goodfellas better than Casino?

1. Goodfellas is better than Casino.
1.1. Pro: Casino uses too much narration.
1.2. Pro: [Goodfellas](https://www.rottentomatoes.com/m/1032176_goodfellas?) has a higher Rotten Tomatoes score than [Casino](https://www.rottentomatoes.com/m/1067987_casino?)
1.2.1. Con: Popularity does not necessarily suggest quality.
1.2.2. Con: Casino made over [twice as much](https://www.the-numbers.com/movies/custom-comparisons/Goodfellas/Casino) at the worldwide box office than Goodfellas, suggesting it is the more popular movie.
1.2.3. Pro: Rotten Tomatoes is set by renowned critics who know a lot about film in general.
1.3. Pro: Casino is a rip-off of Goodfellas as far as the narrative structure is concerned.
1.3.1. Con: That doesn't necessarily mean that Goodfellas did it better.
1.4. Pro: Goodfellas has a better supporting cast.
1.4.1. Pro: Robert De Niro is a great actor.
1.4.1.1. Pro: He has received [several awards and nominations](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/List_of_awards_and_nominations_received_by_Robert_De_Niro), including the Lifetime Achievement Award or Man of the Year.
1.5. Con: Casino has a better ending.
1.6. Con: Robert DeNiro is a better lead than Ray Liotta
1.7. Con: Casino has a better anti-drugs message.
1.7.1. Con: The abundance of drug use throughout the film desensitises viewers to drug use.
1.8. Con: The setting of Casino is more dynamic and interesting than Goodfellas.
