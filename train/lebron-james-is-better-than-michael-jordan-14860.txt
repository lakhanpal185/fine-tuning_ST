Discussion Title: Lebron James is better than Michael Jordan.

1. Lebron James is better than Michael Jordan.
1.1. Pro: LeBron James has faced much better competition in the finals.
1.1.1. Con: MJ never had a teammate average more than 22ppg in a final. Lebron has had Kyrie Irving and Dwyane Wade, who both averaged more than 25ppg in the finals they won with Lebron.
1.2. Con: Lebron colluded with other players to form a super team.
1.2.1. Con: The Cavs and Miami, post Lebron, were much worse without him. Bulls won almost the same number of games in the regular season upon Jordan's first retirement.
1.2.2. Pro: Lebron had Dwayne Wade & Ray Allen for his Heat Championship teams, and Kyrie Irving & Kevin Love for his Cavaliers Championship team
1.2.3. Con: Jordan had Scottie Pippen with him for all 6 Championships.
1.3. Con: Lebron never won MVP and DPOY in the same year. Only MJ \('87-'88\) and Hakeem Olajuwon \('93-'94\) both did.
1.4. Con: Jordan was successful in an era where the game was much more physical than it is today.
1.4.1. Pro: [Lebron James](http://www.basketballwithinborders.com/lebron-james-wants-the-game-to-be-more-physical-like-the-90s/) himself 'wishes' the league was more physical as it was in the 90s, giving him an advantage given his stature and current ability.
1.5. Con: Jordan has 5 [MVP awards](http://www.nba.com/history/awards/mvp) and Lebron has four.
1.6. Pro: Lebron has played in 15 consecutive seasons.
1.6.1. Pro: Jordan retired after 93 missing two full seasons.
1.6.1.1. Con: Being a better player is not dependent on playing consecutively, but how well he played while he was active in the NBA.
1.6.1.2. Con: [Jordan took a break from the NBA because his father died...](https://dawindycity.com/2014/06/15/michael-jordan-emotional-fathers-day/)
